60 Years of History In One Milkshake.

Our founder, Zacharias Vretos Panaretos and his wife Theodora took started Parry's Milkbar in 1958 with the sole purpose of creating happiness in the Caringbah community through milkshakes.

Fastforward to today, between renovations and ownership changes, we still pride ourselves on the amazing service that was offered 60 years ago, whipping up delicious milkshakes, and supplying the exotic candies originating overseas.
